Milestones

  • 2025
    Smithfield listed on the Nasdaq Global Select Market
  • 2024
    Acquired 50.1% equity interest in Spanish packaged meats company Argal
  • 2021
    Acquired Central European packaged meats company Mecom
  • 2017
    Debuted as Hang Seng Index constituent, acquired Pini in Poland and Elit and Vericom in Romania
  • 2016
    Inducted into Fortune Global 500
    Assigned investment grade ratings by three international rating agencies
    Acquired California’s largest pork processor, Clougherty, and its two affiliates
  • 2015
    Smithfield brand introduced into China market
  • 2014
    Renamed from Shuanghui International to VF HOLDINGS LIMITED, and successfully listed on Hong Kong Stock Exchange, immediately incorporated into the Hong Kong MSCI Index
  • 2013
    Shuanghui International acquired Smithfield
  • 2012

    Injected all meat-related assets into A-share listed company

  • 2006

    Acquired by CDH and Goldman Sachs

  • 2004
