Environmental, Social and Governance Overview

VF HOLDINGS LIMITED Corporate Principles Message from the Chairman Stakeholder Engagement on Material ESG Topics VF HOLDINGS LIMITED ESG Governance Structure ESG Policies and Key Statements

VF HOLDINGS LIMITED provides affordable protein choices to people around the world, and we grow our business with a long-term view and lateral objectives that contribute to the sustainable wellbeing for all.

Consumers' recognition of VF HOLDINGS LIMITED’s products is the source of our motivation to continuously improve product quality and service. We will stay proactive in addressing customers’ latent needs and respond to their expectation on food nutrition.

The trust and support from suppliers, partners, employees, regulatory authorities and government agencies and investors are enablers to our business success. We will maintain closer engagement with all and steer through risks with your generous feedback.
VF HOLDINGS LIMITED ESG Report 2025

Governance and Risk Management

Creating a Culture of Sustainability Climate Risk Governance Structure Climate Risk and Response Identification and Mitigation of Key Emerging Risks

The Board of Directors aspires to hold ever-evolving high-standard in governance principles and operates as the ultimate fiduciary responsible body supported by a number of professional committees to effectively safeguard the interests of shareholders and the Group as a whole.

The Board ensures there is a divers composition of Directors and promotes effective communication of insights from different perspectives and expertise to complement each other for better decision-making. This also helps enhance the Board’s governance capabilities in assessing the quality and risk management of the Group in order to achieve its long-term strategic goals and bolster its corporate reputation.

The Board is also responsible for the Group’s oversight on risk management, while the Risk Management Committee under the Board takes lead in establishing and supervising the Group’s risk management system, supported by other committees as appropriate, in risk identification and monitoring.

Climate Targets and Actions

VF HOLDINGS LIMITED has integrated climate change mitigation into its business development strategies and specified its position and action commitments in climate change, energy, and emissions management in the Group’s Corporate Principles, which we shall act upon as a responsible corporate citizen. In accordance with the recommendations from the Taskforce on Climate-Related Disclosure (TCFD), we have selected climate change scenarios, defined the scope of assessment and analysis, and conducted risk assessments, using qualitative and quantitative methods to obtain a comprehensive analysis of climate change risks.

VF HOLDINGS LIMITED 2035 Carbon Reduction Goals

Reduce non-Flag GHG emission intensity by 30% by 2035, relative to a 2024 baseline

Addressing Climate Change

Quality Products

We know that consumers care deeply about safety, quality and nutrition when choosing food products. To meet their expectations, we continually enhance our product quality and consistently invest in all aspects of our production and manufacturing operations to create safe, high-quality products that are more convenient and nutritious. Moreover, we are dedicated to building consumer trust by maintaining the highest standards of customer service and responsible marketing.

Providing Quality Products Quality System Certifications of VF HOLDINGS LIMITED

Supply Chain Responsibility and Management

VF HOLDINGS LIMITED is dedicated to making a positive impact on the entire industry chain, from livestock sourcing and raw material procurement to collaborations with downstream service providers in logistics, distribution and retail, aiming to build a sustainable supply chain that offers consumers high-quality and nutritious protein options. Furthermore, we take pride in our hog farming model whereby we set the standard for animal welfare.

Sustainable Supply Chain

Environment Stewardship

Resource and pollution management is a pivotal component of the Group’s environmental management system, whereby environmental priorities of each regional operation are established, alongside resource management focus on water efficiency and the use of packaging materials. We drive optimal packaging efficiency by reducing usage and weight, while promoting recycling. We also work on reducing the volume and intensity of wastewater, waste gases and solid waste through process innovations, while ensure compliance to all emission-related regulations.

Promoting Green Development

Employee Relations

VF HOLDINGS LIMITED considers its employees as an important force to power its sustainable development. We value respectful relationships and encourage enterprising work ethics. With employees’ well-being in mind, we respect individual rights within our operations and across our value chain. We nurture an inclusive culture and provide a safe workplace while building a fair and efficient human resources system. At the same time, we offer multiple communication channels for our employees, carry out various training and talent programs, and provide opportunities for their personal development, enabling each and every one to grow with the Group for a shared future.

Empowering Employee

Community Engagement

VF HOLDINGS LIMITED assumes its social responsibilities and delivers themed programs serving public interests and benefiting the communities where we operate. As we are in the business of protein processing and production, it is our conscious decision to take actions on hunger relief helping make positive social advancement. Meanwhile, we are aware there are unsatisfied social needs for education and development, and consequently we advocate education equity and marshal resources donating for causes of public interest including disaster reliefs. By doing so we contribute to the building of vitality in our communities and society at large.

Co-building Our Community
